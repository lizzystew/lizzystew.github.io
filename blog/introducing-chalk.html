Introducing chalk

Chalk is a high quality, completely customisable, performant and 100% free blog template for Jekyll.

October 17, 2016 - 1 minute read -
web jekyll

Download Chalk here!

Features:

  • Dark and Light theme.
  • Filter on tags.
  • customisable pagination.
  • Beautified link sharing in Facebook and other social media.
  • Automatic time to read post indicator.
  • Automatic RSS feed.
  • About page.
  • 404 page.
  • SEO optimized.
  • PageSpeed optimized.
  • Cross browser support (supports all modern browsers).
  • Media embed for videos.
  • Enlarge images on click (like Medium).

Integrations

  • Google Analytics
  • Google Fonts
  • Disqus
  • Ionicons
  • Social media links

Used tools

  • Autoprefixer
  • Bower
  • Circle CI
  • Html-proofer
  • Jekyll
  • Jekyll assets
  • Jekyll Sitemap
  • HTML5 Boilerplate (Influenced by)
  • Kickster
  • Retina.js
  • STACSS
